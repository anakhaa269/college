About Us

SNGCE is a premier institution located in Kadayiruppu, Kolenchery, Ernakulam. Our college is committed to nurturing future engineers through quality education, research, and innovation. With a serene campus and highly qualified faculty, we aim to create professionals who contribute to the development of society.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech - Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.Tech - Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech - Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech - Civil Engineering
  • B.Tech - Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • MBA - Master of Business Administration

Facilities

  • Well-equipped Laboratories
  • Digital Library and e-Resources
  • Wi-Fi Enabled Campus
  • Separate Hostels for Boys and Girls
  • Canteen and Cafeteria
  • Transportation Facilities
  • Sports and Fitness Center

Admission

Admissions to B.Tech programs are conducted through the Kerala Engineering Entrance Examination (KEAM). For MBA, students must qualify in entrance exams like CMAT/KMAT/KU MAT. Interested candidates can apply online through our official website or contact the admission office.
